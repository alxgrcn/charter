# Vet Center Counseling

## Program Overview
Vet Centers are community-based counseling centers operated by the VA's Readjustment Counseling Service. They provide free, confidential counseling in a non-clinical, non-hospital setting. Vet Centers are staffed largely by fellow veterans and are designed to feel approachable and low-barrier.

Vet Centers are separate from VA Medical Centers and do NOT require VA Healthcare enrollment to access.

Services provided include:
- Individual counseling
- Group counseling
- Family counseling
- Military sexual trauma (MST) counseling and assessment
- Bereavement counseling
- Referral to VA Medical Center or community services
- 24/7 call center staffed by fellow veterans: 1-877-WAR-VETS (1-877-927-8387)

## Who This May Help
Veterans and service members who:
- Served in a combat theater or area of hostility
- Experienced military sexual trauma (MST) — any era, any discharge status
- Are seeking counseling without going through VA enrollment
- Want to talk with someone who has shared military experience
- Are not yet ready for a full VA healthcare appointment

## Eligibility
- Any veteran who served on active duty in a combat theater or area of hostility (any era)
- Any veteran or service member who experienced MST — regardless of era of service
- Active duty service members with combat deployment (including Guard and Reserve)
- Family members of eligible veterans for certain services

## Discharge Status
- Standard Vet Center services require honorable or general (under honorable conditions) discharge
- MST counseling is available regardless of discharge status — including OTH discharge veterans
- OTH discharge veterans may be referred by Vet Center staff to VA emergency mental health if needed

## No Enrollment Required
Veterans do NOT need to be enrolled in VA Healthcare to use Vet Center services. You can walk in without a referral or an appointment.

## Cost
All Vet Center services are free of charge.

## How to Access in California
- Walk in to the nearest Vet Center without an appointment
- Call the 24/7 Vet Center Call Center: 1-877-WAR-VETS (1-877-927-8387)
- Use the locator at vetcenter.va.gov

## California Vet Center Locations (Key Locations)
Over 20 Vet Center locations across California, including:
- Los Angeles (multiple locations), Long Beach, Gardena, Inglewood
- Ventura, San Bernardino / Inland Empire
- San Diego, Sacramento, San Jose / Bay Area, Fresno
Full directory: vetcenter.va.gov

## Key Message for Veterans
If walking into a VA hospital feels like too much right now, a Vet Center may be the right first step. You don't need any paperwork or prior enrollment to show up. The 24/7 call line connects you with a fellow veteran any time of day or night.
